Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this tool free for commercial use?

Yes! The tool itself is free. However, the copyright of the specific voice depends on the provider (Google, Microsoft, etc.). Generally, system voices are safe for YouTube monetization.

Why do I see different voices on my phone vs laptop?

This tool fetches voices installed on your specific device. Android phones usually have Google voices, while Windows laptops have Microsoft David/Zira. iOS devices will show Siri-based voices.

Can I convert Hindi text to speech?

Yes! If your device has a Hindi voice pack installed (like "Google Hindi"), simply type in Hindi, select the Hindi voice from the dropdown, and it will speak perfectly.
